# Dashboard (Streamlit) Spec

**Pages**
1. **Today**: open positions, orders, risk status, session indicator.
2. **Performance**: cumulative PnL, weekly PnL vs +$50 target, drawdown, Sharpe, hit rate.
3. **Sessions**: bar charts per-session PnL/hit-rate; slippage & spread heatmaps.
4. **Watchlist**: table of current candidates with filters and eligibility flags.
5. **Diagnostics**: feature importances, confusion matrix, regime hit rates.
6. **Journal**: trades with AI summaries, weekly retrospective notes.
